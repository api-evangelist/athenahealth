A guide outlining a monthly five-minute audit procedure for maintaining medical practice Google Business Profiles. While 84% of patients check online reviews before selecting providers, most profiles drift out of date without regular maintenance; the audit covers six checks: business info accuracy, hours, photos, services listing, review responses, and Q&A monitoring.
